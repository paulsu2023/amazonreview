What This Is About

We work with real shoppers who can try products and share honest, real-use opinions.

What is an Amazon review?

An Amazon review is a public opinion from a shopper about what the product is really like after use: quality, size, comfort, setup, packaging, and value.

Why do reviews matter?

For a new product, real reviews help buyers make decisions. They also help us find unclear instructions, quality issues, or details we should improve.

What can testers receive?

Selected testers may receive a free product opportunity or campaign support through PayPal or Amazon gift card, confirmed clearly before participation.

Why is it safe?

You can contact us through WhatsApp, Facebook, email, or Instagram. We confirm details before you join, and eligible Amazon orders still follow Amazon's normal return options.

How to Find Your Amazon Profile

After you join the WhatsApp or Facebook group, we may ask you to send your Amazon public profile so we can verify you are a real Amazon shopper. Do not send your password, order details, or payment information.

  1. Sign in to Amazon and open amazon.com/gp/profile.
  2. If that does not open directly, go to Account and look for Your Profile.
  3. Open your public profile page, copy the browser address, and send that link to us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are real product reviews important?

Real reviews help shoppers understand the product and help us improve quality, packaging, sizing, and instructions. We only want your honest experience after you actually use the product.

Is there any cost to join?

No application fee. Selected testers receive product access for the campaign they join.

How are testers selected?

We match based on product fit, location, availability, and previous testing reliability.

Do I need to submit my address here?

No. This page does not collect your address or profile information. Product details and next steps are handled through our WhatsApp or Facebook group.

Why do you ask for social and Amazon profile links?

We no longer collect them on this page. After you join the group, send your Amazon public profile to us directly following the group instructions. Never share passwords, order details, or payment information.

Can I apply for multiple campaigns?

Yes, but we may limit active campaigns per tester so feedback quality stays high.
